Every Student Should

Student progress will always remain a key priority because outcomes so often dictate future pathways and opportunities for young people. The curriculum at Hove Park School is not just about the sequencing of lessons and subjects across the Key Stages but also the half termly project days and extra-curricular provision that together enrich and broaden a student’s time at Hove Park. Our vision is about identifying the skills, values and attributes that we believe students should develop during their time with us and the things they will see, hear, try and explore. In essence our vision is to provide a whole education for all our students.

Mental Health and Wellbeing
